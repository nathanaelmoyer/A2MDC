X - Missing. Tooth extracted during life of individual.
V - Unrestored virgin tooth. Tooth may have caries but no restoration.
C - Crown. Any laboratory processed restoration such as a gold crown or porcelain crown.
R -  Root canal. Any type of endontic procedure that has been performed on the tooth.
